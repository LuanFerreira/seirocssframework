Buttons

Buttons have a few options for customization:

  • Change colors with s-default, s-primary, s-danger, s-warning, s-success, and s-info
  • Change sizes with btn-small, btn-large

Small Buttons


<button class="button btn-small">Default</button>
<button class="button-primary btn-small">Primary</button>
<button class="button-danger btn-small">Danger</button>
<button class="button-warning btn-small">Warning</button>
<button class="button-success btn-small">Success</button>
<button class="button-info btn-small">Info</button>
