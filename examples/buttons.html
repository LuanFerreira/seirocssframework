Buttons

Buttons have a few options for customization:

  • Change colors with s-default, s-primary, s-danger, s-warning, s-success, and s-info
  • Change sizes with btn-small, btn-large

Small Buttons

    
<button class="button s-default btn-small">Default</button>
<button class="button s-primary btn-small">Primary</button>
<button class="button s-danger btn-small">Danger</button>
<button class="button s-warning btn-small">Warning</button>
<button class="button s-success btn-small">Success</button>
<button class="button s-info btn-small">Info</button>
